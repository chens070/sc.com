UX/UI Branding Packaging

X-ZAPZ

X-ZAPZ is a energy drink brand targeting dancers. Outside packaging using a battery shape to symbolize power charging after drinking.

The package comes with 4 different flavors:
1.Original blast
2.Apple pops
3.Lemon Break
4.Strawberry Pirouette
Flavor names was inspired by different dance moves.
